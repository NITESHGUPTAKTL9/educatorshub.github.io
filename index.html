What is Educators Hub ?



Solutions In 9 Languages


Not everyone speaks the same programming language. We give you solutions to every single question in 9 different languages: JavaScript, Python, Swift, C++, Java, C#, Go, Kotlin, and TypeScript.

Code-Execution Environment


Coding out solutions to algorithm problems is the best way to practice. Our code-execution environment lets you type out your answers and run them against our test cases right here on the website.

Space-Time Complexity Analyses


Understanding how much memory an algorithm uses and how fast it runs is exceedingly important. All of our explanations include a rigorous space-time complexity analysis.



4 Curated Assessments


Real coding interviews are timed and have multiple questions. We've carefully curated 4 assessments to reflect what a real day of coding interviews might look like, filled with variety and appropriate difficulty.

Certificate Of Completion


Completing all questions on AlgoExpert is no easy feat. It takes time and effort, and it demonstrates a keen understanding of data structures and algorithms. Completing all AlgoExpert questions earns you a certificate of your coding-interview preparedness.

Experience-Backed Interview Tips


Coding interviews go beyond just algorithms and data structures. Our coding interview tips video series gives you crucial tips and tricks that'll make you stand out from other software engineers.
